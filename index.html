Welcome to Dignans

Welcome to Dignans Catering, where exceptional culinary experiences meet outstanding service.
Located in the heart of Edinburgh, we have spent the past five years delighting customers with our premium outside catering services.
Our commitment to quality food and exceptional service is at the core of everything we do.

Dignans is a renowned outside catering company, operating from our cafe.
Over the years, we have built strong relationships with local businesses and offices around our cafe, offering regular catering services and special event buffets.
Much of our growth is from the positive word of mouth recommendations from our satisfied clients.
